\subsection{Detailed Description}
Parabolic bowl bottom with oscillating flow. 

This test case is to examine the capacity of the methods to treat flooding and drying. Details of this example please refer to Ern et. al (2008).

\mbox{[}1\mbox{]}. Ern A, Piperno S, Djadel K. A well-\/balanced Runge-\/\+Kutta discontinuous Galerkin method for the shallow-\/water equations with flooding and drying. International Journal for Numerical Methods in Fluids 2008;58\+:1â€“25. doi\+:10.\+1002/fld.1674.
